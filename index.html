frequently asked questions

  • Who can come to camp?
    For high school, anyone from incoming grade 7 to incoming grade 12. For college, incoming college to fresh grad.
  • What's included in the camp fee?
    Food, lodging, and fruitful time with the Lord.
  • What do I need to bring?
    Clothes for at least 4 days and 3 nights (include sports and swim wear, jacket or sweater, a dark shirt, rubber shoes), toiletries (towel, soap, toilet paper, etc.), personal medicines, flashlight, bottled water (optional), insect repellant, Bible, pen, notepad, pillows/blankets (optional) and pocket money.
  • What is a buzz group or y-group?
    It is a group of people who journey together in life with the Lord
  • Will I get to meet my buzz group leader and members before camp?
    Yes, we will have a pre-camp fellowship on May 26, 2018, where you can meet your fellow campers and camp leaders.
  • What if I have more questions?
    You can contact us via email at youthlive@gcf.org.ph or text us through +639175327741.
