GCF Youth LIVE is the youth expression of Greenhills Christian Fellowship Ortigas. We are a community of high school and college students committed to Jesus's call to discipleship. We are a family living in view of eternity - a movement for the youth, by the youth
Camp LIVE is the annual summer camp for high school and college students. The four-day gathering of youth from all backgrounds dedicated to encountering Jesus through action-packed activities, journeying with each other through our Y-Groups, and fellowship over God's message for the next generation.
